About Us

At Made2Makeup, we are inspired by compassion for all living beings to direct our customers to products that beautify and protect them and the planet. From VitaminCells enriched Skin Care to FlowerColor Makeup infused with flower wax, we guarantee we can help you find products with truth, beauty and 100% satisfaction.

Since 2017, we have been dedicated to respectful science. “At Made2Makeup we direct your hard-to-find beauty products where we break all the rules.” We do extensive research to find cruelty-free, natural beauty product development. The industry is saturated with product lines that make false claims and take short cuts. We connect you to companies where, they are made ethically, authentically, are safe and offer superior performance. Each active natural ingredient in the products has undergone various clinical trials and studies to validate their benefits in formulas that honor the most sensitive of Intelligent Beauties who are devoted to pampering themselves responsibly.

WE BELIEVE IN CLEAN PRODUCTS

In the pursuit of living more simply, naturally, and conscious of our environment we've put together a guide to our favorite organic beauty brands. Many makeup brands marketed as 'natural' or 'green' are often not actually either of those things - but our site will direct you to organic makeup brands that use pure, earth friendly ingredients and never test their cosmetics on animals. We've created a site that helps match you with these types of products. Made2Makeup is updated regularly, so please enjoy the variety of organic or cruelty free beauty brands!

Gratitude is a number one emotion at Made2Makeup.

Inspired by the philosophy of Eliot Cowan the author of Plant, Spirit, Medicine, we are humbled by and grateful for the contributions of the plant world to our products; our clothing, our homes and our food.

"Made2MakeUP Founded in 2017 by licensed Esthetician Nurah Muhammad, Made2MakeUp is a champion in helping people find natural and organic beauty products. Nurah began Made2Makeup to celebrate the beauty of her fellow humans, because of her disgust with the use of animals for product testing, and with respect for the wonder of nature. To that end, Nurah realized there was a world of plant-based ingredients including powerful nutraceuticals from ethical suppliers. “Every small purchase we each make about what to use on our bodies or what to eat, affects not only us, but the entire planet.”"

Nurah Muhammad CEO-Made2Makeup
